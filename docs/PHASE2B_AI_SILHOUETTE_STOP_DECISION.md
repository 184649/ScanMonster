# Phase 2B AI Silhouette Stop Decision

Status: Final Phase 2B decision record / design process only / 2026-07-18

## 1. Purpose and scope

Phase 2B tested whether the text-prompt-centered, AI-only workflow could produce flagship character silhouettes strong enough to enter Gate 2. The three test motifs were hamster, elephant, and snake. Eight black-and-white silhouette concepts were attempted for each motif. The work did not authorize a production character, official name, character ID, image, rarity, or `releaseStatus` change.

## 2. Observed result

Across the three motifs, the outputs converged on differences in pose, orientation, body proportion, and familiar animal-pictogram contours. They remained variations of recognizable animals or biological icons rather than independent character proposals.

The silhouettes did not establish all of the following at the required level:

- a concise character core;
- a personality expressed by construction and posture;
- a motif-specific reason to like the individual;
- a memorable identity that would survive removal of face, color, texture, and equipment;
- sufficient structural distinction from the other options.

Differences in outer contour alone are not sufficient evidence to proceed to Gate 2. Technical cleanliness and species readability do not substitute for the user's interest in seeing a character completed.

## 3. User decision

| Motif | Gate 1 decision |
|---|---|
| Hamster | `ALL_REJECTED` |
| Elephant | `ALL_REJECTED` |
| Snake | `ALL_REJECTED` |

**Gate 1 result:** No silhouette created sufficient interest to proceed to Gate 2.

This is the explicit stop condition defined in Phase 2A. No option is promoted, ranked, or treated as a latent production candidate.

## 4. Stop decision

The same text-prompt-centered AI-only method will not be regenerated or repeated for these flagship decisions. AI-only character design is stopped at Gate 1, and WORLDAWN will move to human-authored reference-character and IP-language design.

This decision does not claim that automated generation can never contribute to the project. After a human designer has produced approved baseline characters, model sheets, and cross-character rules, separately reviewed AI-assisted production or exploration may be considered. Any such use requires rights, contract, quality, and process review; it is not authorized by this record.

## 5. Repository and production impact

At the start of Phase 2C, the remote Phase 2B branch `art/flagship-silhouette-validation` existed at `818fbc73c50b307514d1a3f3a6bb296c090b265c`, but no `hamster_silhouettes.png`, `elephant_silhouettes.png`, or `snake_silhouettes.png` was present in the repository. No Phase 2B image is therefore available to promote or register.

No Phase 2B output has been added to official art paths, manifests, catalogs, server seeds, the database, or the application. Production data, existing IDs, discovery history, official discovery numbers, rarity, and `releaseStatus` remain unchanged.

## 6. Next route

Phase 2C prepares a human-character-designer commission package. The initial paid test begins with Stage 0 creative direction and Stage 1 black silhouettes. It does not begin with finished color illustrations. Stage 2 and later stages remain blocked until the user can select a silhouette.
